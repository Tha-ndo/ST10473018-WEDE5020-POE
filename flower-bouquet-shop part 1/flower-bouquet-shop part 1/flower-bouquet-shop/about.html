About BLOOM&CO

BLOOM&CO is a boutique floral studio built on passion, creativity and the joy of transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary ones. What began as a small home workspace has grown into a beloved floral brand known for elegance, authenticity and care.

“Every flower is a story waiting to be told — and we tell yours with love.”

Our Journey

2018

BLOOM&CO begins as a small home-based flower studio with weekend deliveries.

2020

We moved into our first boutique shop and introduced custom bouquet services.

2022

Launched our seasonal collection and partnered with local growers for fresh supply.

2024

Expanded to weddings, events, and corporate floral styling.

Our Values

Quality

We use only premium blooms sourced from trusted local growers.

Creativity

Every bouquet is designed with artistic detail and a unique personal touch.

Care

We put heart into everything we do — from crafting to customer service.
